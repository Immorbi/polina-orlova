01 Bio

Textiles  ·  Installation  ·  Material Transformation

Ukrainian multidisciplinary artist working with textiles, installation, and material transformation — including salt, mirror surfaces, and drip-dyeing systems. In her practice, she explores the "architecture of tension" — moments where strict structure meets the unpredictability of the material and the process of decay.

02 Artist
Statement

My practice explores the tension between constructed structure and the independent will of the material. I create situations in which a work cannot remain static, allowing time, chemical processes, light, or physical stress to alter its form.

I often use applied materials and subject them to deliberate damage — bending, cutting, dissolving, or corroding — as a way to release unexpected qualities and generate new, unstable states. Through this process the object shifts from a fixed form to something closer to a condition or event.

The viewer encounters a sense of instability, as if the work could change or collapse at any moment. Social context appears only indirectly, reflected in systems of pressure, resistance, and adaptation that shape both materials and the structures surrounding us.
